Narendra Modi is a prominent Indian politician and the current Prime Minister of India.

Birth:
Narendra Modi was born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, India.

Political Career:
Modi started his political career as a member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) and later joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014.

Achievements:
- Modi played a key role in the economic development of Gujarat during his tenure as Chief Minister.
- He became the Prime Minister of India in May 2014 and was re-elected in May 2019.

Reforms:
Modi's government has implemented various reforms in areas such as taxation, education, and healthcare.

Foreign Relations:
He has been actively involved in shaping India's foreign relations and has engaged with several world leaders.
